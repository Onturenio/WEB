Current position

I'm a climate researcher, and my activity focuses on the use of climate models. My interest is the long-term evolution of the climate, and how external forcings (anthropogenic or not) play a role on it. I have currently a postdoc researcher position at the Helmholtz-Zentrum Geesthacht (Germany). In particular, I work in the Paleoclimate Group of the Institute for Coastal Research, where I'm conducting a number of palaeoclimate simulations to get insight into the evolution of precipitation over Europe during the last 2k years.

Research fields

  • Regional Climate Modelling
  • Climate Change Projections and uncertainties
  • Paleoclimatology
  • Internal vs. forced variability
